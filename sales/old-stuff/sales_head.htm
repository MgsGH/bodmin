Welcome to SkOF's and Falsterbo B.O.'s webshop.

Very many activities at SkOF and Falsterbo B.O. relays on voluntary efforts and work with little or no economic benefit.
By purchasing books, postcards, stickers binoculars and other items from our webshop you make a contribution to the protection of birds in southern Sweden and to the work at Falsterbo Bird Observatory. You order your items here and then you pay at delivery. Invoice comes with the ordered items. N.B. Postage is always extra.
